### Textbooks

**1. Digital Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Chapter 4 (Combinational Logic)<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of multiplexer circuits, data selection, and combinational logic design._

**2. Digital Logic and Computer Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano - Chapter 4<br/>
_Fundamental concepts of digital logic design including multiplexers and data routing systems._

**3. Digital Electronics: Principles, Devices and Applications**<br/>
BY: Anil K. Maini - Chapter 6<br/>
_Practical approach to digital circuit design with detailed analysis of multiplexer and demultiplexer circuits._

**4. Digital Systems: Principles and Applications**<br/>
BY: Ronald J. Tocci, Neal S. Widmer, and Gregory L. Moss - Chapter 9<br/>
_Modern approach to digital systems design including MSI logic circuits and multiplexer applications._

**5. Fundamentals of Digital Logic with VHDL Design**<br/>
BY: Stephen Brown and Zvonko Vranesic - Chapter 6<br/>
_Advanced coverage of combinational circuits with emphasis on multiplexer design and implementation._
