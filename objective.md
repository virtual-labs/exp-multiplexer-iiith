The objective of this experiment is to design and analyze different multiplexer circuits. You will be able to design 2X1 multiplexers, 4X1 multiplexers, and understand hierarchical multiplexer construction.

- **2X1 Multiplexer**: Design a basic data selector circuit with single select line
- **4X1 Multiplexer**: Implement a complete four-input multiplexer using 2X1 multiplexers
- **Hierarchical Design**: Construct larger multiplexers using smaller multiplexer building blocks

Through this experiment, students will gain practical experience in digital circuit design and understand the fundamental principles of data selection and routing implementation in hardware.
